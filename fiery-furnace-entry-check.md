PERMIT COVERS ENTRY — no action needed.

Arches National Park eliminated its timed-entry vehicle reservation system entirely for 2026. No separate timed-entry slot is required to enter the park on May 7, 2026 (or any day in 2026). The Self-Guided Fiery Furnace Exploration permit (already confirmed) is the only advance booking needed beyond the America the Beautiful pass for park admission.
